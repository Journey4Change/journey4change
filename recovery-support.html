When Cravings Hit

Coping Strategies

Keep these in your mind. Practice them before you need them. They work.

01

Call Someone

Pick up the phone before you pick up. Call a peer mentor, sponsor, family member, or the 0800 12 13 14 helpline. Connection breaks cravings.

02

Move Your Body

Walk, run, do push-ups, stretch. Physical movement releases endorphins that naturally reduce cravings and anxiety within minutes.

03

Change the Scene

Remove yourself from the trigger environment immediately. Go to a different room, walk outside, go to a shop — location change breaks the craving cycle.

04

Breathe Through It

Use the 4-7-8 breathing tool. Controlled breathing activates your parasympathetic nervous system, reducing the physical sensation of craving.

05

Remember Your Why

Write your three biggest reasons for staying sober on a card. Read it now. Your family. Your health. Your future. Let those reasons be louder than the craving.

06

Delay for 20 Minutes

Tell yourself you will wait 20 minutes before doing anything. Cravings peak and pass. Most urges fade on their own if you ride them out.
