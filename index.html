Who We Are

Our Mission

Journey4Change exists to break the stigma around mental health in South Africa by travelling across all 9 provinces — sharing stories, offering hope, and creating safe spaces for healing and conversation.

Through this journey, we connect with individuals, communities, and organisations, bringing light to the struggles many face in silence and reminding them that they are not alone.
