Founder & Director

Euclid Tumelo
Ramaila

Limpopo, South Africa  ·  Journey4Change, Est. 2025

Euclid Tumelo Ramaila is the founder and driving force of Journey4Change — a bold mental health awareness initiative that takes him across all 9 provinces of South Africa, covering over 3,000 km on foot. His mission is simple yet profound: to break the stigma surrounding mental health, one conversation and one kilometre at a time.

Born and raised in Limpopo, Euclid knows firsthand the silence that surrounds mental illness in many South African communities. This journey is his answer to that silence — a declaration that no one should suffer alone, and that healing is possible when people feel seen and heard.

Through his travels, Euclid plans to partner with schools, clinics, churches, and community organisations, creating safe spaces for open dialogue about depression, anxiety, trauma, and the everyday mental health challenges people face but rarely speak about.
