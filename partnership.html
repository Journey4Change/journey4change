A Little About Me

Founder, Journey4Change

Walking 3,500km to raise mental health awareness across South Africa

Journey4Change started as a personal mission and grew into a movement — a 3,500 km journey across South Africa's provinces to break the silence around mental health, gender-based violence, and substance abuse.

I believe real change happens through partnership. No single person or organisation can carry this work alone, which is why I'm always open to connecting with people and organisations who share this vision — whether that means a formal partnership, a joint campaign, a speaking invitation, or simply an extra pair of hands along the road.
