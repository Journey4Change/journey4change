South African Resources

South Africa has several dedicated organizations providing depression support in all 11 official languages.

SADAG (SA Depression & Anxiety Group)

Africa's largest mental health support and advocacy group with 24/7 helplines.

Website → Helpline: 0800 456 789 or 0800 567 567 WhatsApp: 087 163 2025 SMS: 31393

Lifeline South Africa

National counselling service with crisis support and face-to-face counselling centers.

Website → Helpline: 0861 322 322 WhatsApp: 065 989 9238

The South African Federation for Mental Health

Advocacy group promoting mental healthcare access across all provinces.

Website → 011 781 1852

Akeso Clinics

Network of private mental health clinics offering specialized depression treatment.

Website → 24hr: 0861 435 787
