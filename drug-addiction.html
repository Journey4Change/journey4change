Who We Are

Our Mission

Journey4Change extends its mission to the hidden crisis of drug addiction across South Africa. We walk alongside individuals trapped by substance use and the families who love them bringing hope, practical support, and a clear path to recovery.

Through community outreach, peer-led support groups, and partnerships with certified treatment centres across all 9 provinces, we connect people to care without judgement and without shame.

Addiction does not discriminate. Whether you are in a city, a township, or a rural village we are working to bring help to your door.

All services are free, confidential, and judgment-free.

How We Help

Our Approach

Reach Out

The first step is the hardest. Our trained counsellors are available to listen, guide, and connect you with the right support no waiting lists, no judgement.

Build a Plan

Together we create a personalised recovery roadmap whether that means inpatient rehab, outpatient counselling, NA meetings, or community support circles.

Stay Connected

Recovery does not end after treatment. Our aftercare network, peer mentors, and family support programmes walk with you for the long journey ahead.
